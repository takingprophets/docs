## Overview

**Flow State Model** is an all-in-one framework indicator that surfaces the core elements of the TakingProphets trading model on a single chart. It combines Fair Value Gap detection, Change in State of Delivery (CISD) detection, multi-timeframe FVG analysis, and an interactive trade checklist into one overlay. The name reflects the goal: putting you in a flow state where every critical piece of information is visible at once, so execution feels natural rather than rushed.

The indicator runs FVG detection using ATR-based sensitivity thresholds to filter out noise, gives you a configurable lookback period so your chart isn't overwhelmed with historical data, and overlays CISD markers whenever a liquidity sweep followed by displacement occurs. A table in the corner displays the current state of each checklist item — HTF PDA, aligned timeframe, liquidity sweep, SMT, and CISD — letting you check confluence at a glance before entering.

Internally, the model also references the 4-hour, Daily, and Weekly FVGs to frame macro delivery context, though these are managed automatically without requiring manual timeframe switching.

## Settings

### General Settings

**Detection Sensitivity** — `High` | `Medium` | `Low` (default: `Medium`)

Controls how small an FVG needs to be to register. `High` catches very small gaps using a 0.2× ATR minimum. `Medium` requires 0.5× ATR — a reasonable filter that removes most noise. `Low` only shows large, impulsive gaps at 1.0× ATR. If you're seeing too many tiny gaps, move to `Low`. If valid setups are being missed, move to `High`.

---

**Lookback Period** — `1 Day` | `1 Week` | `1 Month` | `Max` (default: `1 Week`)

How far back the indicator looks when drawing FVGs and CISDs. `1 Week` is the standard — it keeps the chart clean while providing enough historical reference. `Max` shows all available data and is best used for review sessions, not live trading.

## How We Use It

The Flow State Model is the team's primary chart overlay during live sessions. On a 1-minute chart, it surfaces every CISD and FVG in real time so there's no manual marking needed. The checklist serves as a pre-trade discipline tool — before entering any setup, the team runs through the five items (HTF PDA, aligned timeframe, liquidity sweep, SMT, CISD) and only takes trades where at least three or four are confirmed.

The CISD detections in particular are the entry trigger — when the model marks a CISD after a sweep, and the FVG from the same displacement zone is still unmitigated above or below, that's the setup. The checklist table in the corner keeps the analysis visible throughout the session without needing to look away from price.
