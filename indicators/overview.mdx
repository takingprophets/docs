All indicators are tested and actively used during our **live trading sessions (9–11 AM ET, Mon–Fri)**. If you want to see them in action before diving into the docs, that's the best place to start.

## Instrument Focus

Our indicators are optimized for **CME futures**, specifically:

- **ES** — E-mini S&P 500
- **NQ** — E-mini NASDAQ-100
- **MES** — Micro E-mini S&P 500
- **MNQ** — Micro E-mini NASDAQ-100

They can be applied to other instruments, but the settings and default calibrations are tuned for the above.
