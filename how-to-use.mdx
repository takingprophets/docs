## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A **TradingView account** — a [TradingView Essentials plan](https://www.tradingview.com/pricing/) or higher is required to use alerts on our indicators
- A **Whop purchase** — indicators are sold through our Whop store; access is granted through there
- Your **TradingView username** — you'll need this during the claim process

---

## Step 1: Purchase on Whop

All Taking Prophets indicators are sold through **Whop**. After completing your purchase, you'll get access to the Taking Prophets Whop community where you'll claim your indicator access.

---

## Step 2: Claim Your TradingView Access

Once inside the Whop community:

1. Open the **claim access app** inside the Taking Prophets Whop page
2. Enter your **TradingView username** exactly as it appears on your TradingView account
3. Submit the form

<Note>
  Your TradingView username is case-sensitive. Double-check it before submitting — it needs to match your account exactly.
</Note>

After submitting, it typically takes **5–10 minutes** for access to be granted to your TradingView account.

---

## Step 3: Find the Indicator in TradingView

Once access has been granted:

1. Open TradingView and log into the account you submitted
2. Click the **Indicators** button at the top of any chart (or press `/`)
3. Go to the **Invite-Only Scripts** tab
4. Search for the indicator by name
5. Click to add it to your chart

---

## Step 4: Configure the Settings

Each indicator has a settings panel accessible by clicking the **gear icon** next to the indicator name on the chart.

Key settings you'll typically find:

- **Timeframe** — Some indicators are designed for specific timeframes (1m, 5m, 15m). The individual indicator docs will specify the recommended TF.
- **Sensitivity / Threshold** — Controls how aggressively signals are generated. Start with the defaults.
- **Visual options** — Toggle labels, lines, background shading, and alerts on/off to match your chart style.

<Tip>
  Start with the default settings and watch them during our **live sessions (9–11 AM ET)**. You'll get a feel for how we use them before tweaking anything.
</Tip>

---

## Step 5: Set Up Alerts (Optional)

Most indicators support TradingView alerts. A **TradingView Essentials plan or higher** is required to use them.

1. Right-click on the chart → **Add Alert** (or press `Alt+A`)
2. Set the **Condition** to the indicator and the specific signal
3. Choose your notification method — browser, mobile push, email, or webhook
4. Click **Create**

---

## Troubleshooting

**I submitted my username but the indicator isn't showing up in Invite-Only Scripts**
Wait 5–10 minutes after submitting. If it still doesn't appear, **close TradingView completely and reopen it** — this refreshes your access and usually resolves it.

**The indicator shows an error on the chart**
This usually means your TradingView account doesn't have access to a required data type (e.g., extended hours or certain exchanges). Try switching the chart to a standard session or reach out in Discord.

**I submitted the wrong TradingView username**
Message us in the `#support` channel on Discord and we'll correct it.
