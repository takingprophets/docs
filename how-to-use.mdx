## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A **TradingView account** (Free or paid — most indicators work on Free, but a Pro account is recommended for multi-indicator layouts)
- An **active Taking Prophets membership** or partner indicator access
- Your **invite link or indicator access code** (sent via email or Discord after joining)

---

## Step 1: Accept the Invite

After joining, you'll receive an invite link that grants you access to our private TradingView scripts.

1. Click the invite link from your email or Discord DM
2. You'll be prompted to log in to TradingView if you haven't already
3. Once accepted, the indicator(s) will appear in your **Invite-Only Scripts** library

<Note>
  The invite is tied to your TradingView username. Make sure you're logged into the correct account before clicking the link.
</Note>

---

## Step 2: Add the Indicator to a Chart

1. Open any chart in TradingView
2. Click the **Indicators** button at the top of the chart (or press `/`)
3. Go to the **Invite-Only Scripts** tab
4. Search for the indicator by name (e.g., "Taking Prophets Suite")
5. Click to add it to your chart

---

## Step 3: Configure the Settings

Each indicator has a settings panel accessible by clicking the **gear icon** next to the indicator name on the chart.

Key settings you'll typically find:

- **Timeframe** — Some indicators are designed for specific timeframes (1m, 5m, 15m). The description will specify the recommended TF.
- **Sensitivity / Threshold** — Controls how aggressively signals are generated. Start with defaults.
- **Visual options** — Toggle labels, lines, background shading, and alerts on/off to match your chart style.

<Tip>
  Start with the default settings and watch them in context during our **live sessions (9–11 AM ET)**. You'll get a feel for how we use them before tweaking anything.
</Tip>

---

## Step 4: Set Up Alerts (Optional)

Most indicators support TradingView alerts so you don't have to stare at the screen.

1. Right-click on the chart → **Add Alert** (or press `Alt+A`)
2. Set the **Condition** to the indicator and the specific signal (e.g., "Bullish Signal")
3. Choose your notification method — browser, mobile push, email, or webhook
4. Click **Create**

---

## Troubleshooting

**I don't see the indicator in Invite-Only Scripts**
Make sure you clicked the invite link while logged into TradingView. If it still doesn't appear, message us in the `#support` channel on Discord.

**The indicator shows an error on the chart**
This usually means your TradingView account doesn't have access to a required data type (e.g., extended hours, certain exchanges). Try switching the chart to a standard session or reach out in Discord.

**My invite expired**
Invite links are tied to your membership status. If your membership lapsed, renew it and we'll reissue the invite.
