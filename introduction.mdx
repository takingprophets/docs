## What is Taking Prophets?

Taking Prophets is a software company that builds custom **Pine Script indicators** for futures day trading communities. We partner with trading influencers to deliver precision tools directly to their audiences — then back it up with a live trading community every morning.

Our indicators are built specifically for futures markets (ES, NQ, MNQ, MES, and more) and are designed to give traders a structured edge — not noise.

Taking Prophets is built for traders who are serious about futures — people who want tools with a clear logic behind them, not black-box systems. Whether you're trading ES, NQ, or the micros, our indicators are designed to work with your process, not replace it.
