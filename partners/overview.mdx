## How Partnerships Work

Taking Prophets partners with futures trading influencers on TikTok and Instagram to co-develop custom Pine Script indicators tailored to their specific trading approach. Each partnership produces a set of indicators that reflects that creator's methodology — built by our team, branded for their community.

Revenue is shared between Taking Prophets and the partner, and access to each partner's indicators is sold directly to their audience.

---

## Current Partners

Partner indicator documentation is organized by partnership. Each partner section includes:

- **Strategy overview** — The trading approach the indicators are built around
- **Indicator breakdown** — What each tool does within that system
- **Setup guide** — How to install and configure for that partner's workflow
- **Support** — Where to get help (typically the partner's Discord or community)

## For Prospective Partners

If you're a futures trading influencer with an engaged audience and a defined trading methodology, Taking Prophets can build you a custom indicator suite.

Here's how it works:

1. **Intro call** — We learn your strategy and what tools would serve your audience best
2. **Development** — We build the indicators in Pine Script, iterate with your feedback
3. **Launch** — You sell access to your audience, we handle infrastructure
4. **Revenue split** — Typically 50/50, paid monthly

We currently have **three active partnerships** and are selectively expanding. If you're interested, reach out via [Discord](https://discord.gg/takingprophets) or email us through the site.

---

## Getting Access to Partner Indicators

Partner indicator access is separate from the core Taking Prophets membership. Access is typically sold through the partner's own community and comes with its own TradingView invite link.

If you purchased access through a partner and need help with setup, see the [How to Use](/how-to-use) guide — the process is the same as our in-house indicators.
