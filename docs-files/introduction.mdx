# Welcome to Taking Prophets

Taking Prophets builds custom indicators for day trading communities on TradingView. Our indicators are educational tools designed to help traders visualize market concepts, identify key areas of interest, and never miss a setup with built-in alerts.

## What Are Our Indicators?

Our indicators are **not signals**. They don't tell you what to trade. They are visual tools that display the exact chart work and analysis that experienced traders use — giving you the ability to see the market the same way your mentor does.

## How They Work

All of our indicators are built on top of [TradingView](https://tradingview.com). Once you're granted access:

1. Open TradingView and navigate to your chart
2. Go to **Indicators** → **Invite-Only Scripts**
3. Select the indicator you have access to
4. It will appear directly on your chart
