# How to Access Your Indicators

## Step 1 — Get Access

After purchasing a membership or indicator package, you will receive an invitation to the TradingView scripts you've been granted access to. This comes via email or through your Discord membership role.

## Step 2 — Open TradingView

Go to [TradingView](https://tradingview.com) and log in to your account. If you don't have one, create a free account — you don't need a paid TradingView plan to use our indicators.

## Step 3 — Add the Indicator to Your Chart

1. Open any chart
2. Click the **Indicators** button at the top of the chart
3. Go to the **Invite-Only Scripts** tab
4. You'll see all indicators you've been granted access to
5. Click any indicator to add it to your chart

## Step 4 — Set Up Alerts (Optional but Recommended)

Alerts notify you when a potential setup forms — even when you're not watching the charts.

1. Right-click on the indicator on your chart
2. Select **Add Alert**
3. Choose the condition you want to be alerted on
4. Set your notification method (email, app notification, SMS)
5. Click **Create**

<Note>
  Alerts are one of the most powerful features of our indicators. We strongly recommend setting them up so you never miss a potential trade setup.
</Note>
